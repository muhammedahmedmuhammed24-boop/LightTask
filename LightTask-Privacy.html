Privacy Policy for OrbitTasks

At OrbitTasks, we respect your privacy and do not collect any personal information.

1. Information Collection

The app does not collect any personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

2. Tasks

The app allows you to create tasks with a title, date, and background color. All of this data is stored locally on your device and is never shared with any third party.

3. Security

We ensure that all data stored on your device remains private and secure. No tracking or external transmission occurs.

4. Advertisements

OrbitTasks is completely free of ads and no advertisements will be added in the future.

5. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this policy when necessary and will inform users if any significant changes occur.

All your data stays on your device only and is never sent to any external server.
